Help Guide (nomadful.io)

Learn the basics

App sections

Home
Here's where you'll find the main features of the app. From the Home tab, you can access breathing routines, sounds, mood logs and journal. Check the "Navigation" guide for more details on how to access each feature.

Discover
In this section, you'll find clips related to mental health by popular and noteworthy content creators and professionals, as well as tutorials to use Nomadful (you're here now).

Account
Take full control of your app experience. Here's where you can manage your data, change your region and language, or delete your data. Check the "Settings" guide to learn more.

Gestures

Ruler interface
Whenever you're starting a breathing routine or logging your mood, you'll see a ruler-like interface on the screen. Firmly swipe sideways to control duration or set the mood.

Heading taps
Tap on the heading of the Home or Discover tabs to switch between features. Find more about this by reading the "Navigation" guide.

Navigation

Heading taps

To change between app features (breathing, sounds, mood logs and journal), just tap the heading at the top left corner of the screen. The heading has an arrow next to it. A dropdown menu will appear after tapping.

Ruler interface

To control breathing session duration or to set your current mood, firmly swipe from right to left or viceversa.

Settings

Your details

Editing your details
To edit your details, just tap the pencil icon at the top right corner of your screen. Alternatively, you can tap the "Edit profile" button at the bottom of your screen.

What to edit
You can edit your name and date of birth.

Language and region

Set app language
To set the language in which Nomadful is displayed, just go to Account > Language.

Set app region
To set the app region and view available mental health services, go to Account > Region. This will change your region throughout the app. If you're travelling and want to check services available in your current location, open the help portal and tap the region beacon in the top right corner of the screen. This will change your region momentarily.

Erase your data

To erase your data go to Account > Erase data. Your Nomadful data is separated into chunks so you can choose to delete specific data. If you wish to delete all your data, tap the "Delete all my data" button. Please note that any tipe of data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Start a breathing session

Start a breathing session

To start a breathing session tap the Home tab, and select Breathing from the dropdown menu by tapping the heading. When opening the app, the first screen you'll see is the breathing screen. Tap the "Start a breathing session" button located at the bottom.

Select duration

To select the duration of your breathing routine, swipe the ruler interface sideways. Learn more about gestures on the "Navigation" guide.

Achievements

Start any breathing session to earn your first achievement. You'll earn more achievements by completing a number of breathing minutes or total breaths.

Breath intervals

While sliding the ruler interface, you'll notice a tooltip with the duration of the breathing session in seconds or minutes. Next to it, a conversion in breath units appears. Each breath takes approximately five seconds.

Log your mood

Mood overview

A simple summary of your mood logs appears at the top of the screen. Here you'll see a sum of your total logs and the number of logs for each mood in particular.

Log your mood

Tap the "Log your mood" button at the bottom of your screen and using the ruler interface select your current mood.

Associations

How to use associations

To associate your mood with life factors, just tap the button with the person icon. It is located next to the "Log" button.

Factors available

You can associate your mood with the following life factors: health, people, and current events.

Help portal

What is the help portal?

Nomadful's help portal brings active and free mental helplines and mental health services in your region and groups them toghether while providing you a short and simple description for each service so you can make the best choice and get the most accurate help possible.

Contact information

Each organisation may offer different ways to contact their services. Contact information appears below the name of the service. There is also a link to their website.

Set portal region

If you're travelling abroad and need help, you can tap the region beacon at the top right corner of your screen to switch your region momentarily and view services wherever you are. Please note that this will only change your region until you close the app. To permanently change your region, go to Account > Region.

Log a journal entry

How to log a journal entry

To log a journal entry, navigate to the Journal screen on the Home tab, and then tap the "New journal entry" at the bottom of your screen. An entry must contain text and mood at the time of writing to be logged. Optionally, you can add up to three photos to each of your entries.

Mood in journal

Mood in your journal entries

All journal entries require a mood to be logged with. Unpleasant entries are represented with a dark blue square, neutral entries are represented with a light blue circle, and pleasant entries are represented with a bright blue pentagon.

Add photos

How to add photos to your journal entries

You can add up to three photos for each journal entry. To do so, tap the button with the photo icon located at the top right corner of the screen. Photos you add to your entries while writing them will appear minimised at the bottom left corner of the screen.

Search entries

Search your journal entries

Entries in your journal can be filtered by date and time, mood, or content. To search for entries, tap the search icon at the top right corner of the screen.

Delete entries

To delete a journal entry, tap the eraser icon next to it. If you wish to delete all journal entries, you can do so by deleting all your data. Learn more on the "Settings" guide.
