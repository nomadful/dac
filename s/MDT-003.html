A body scan is a simple way to connect with your surroundings and understand your feelings, while gaining mindfulness in the process.


What you'll need

To start with your body scan, find a quiet space and silence your phone. You can sit down or lie in a mat and close your eyes.


Steps

1. Focus on your breathing. As with every meditation routine, it is essential to bring your focus to your breath.

2. Scan. To "scan your body" means to bring that focus from your breathing into specific parts of your body. Start with your head and face, and gradually move down. Focus on what each part feels at the moment.

3. Body awareness. Notice your body as one, and take a moment to feel it entirely.

4. Wind down. Take a deep breath and when ready, open your eyes and take some time before continuing with your daily activities.
