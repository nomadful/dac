1. Marinara sauce

The classic, flavourful and simple italian tomato sauce. Ideal for almost any pasta dish.


Ingredients

2 cans of crushed tomatoes
4 garlic cloves (minced)
1 onion (chopped)
A pinch of oregano
A pinch of basil
Salt (to taste)
Pepper (to taste)
Olive oil


2. Alfredo sauce

A rich sauce made with butter, cream and cheese. Goes great with fettuccine.


Ingredients

1/2 cup of unsalted butter
1 cup of heavy cream
1 cup of Parmesan cheese (grated)
Salt (to taste)
Pepper (to taste)


3. Pesto sauce

Fresh sauce made from herbs, garlic and cheese. Perfect for a light pasta dish.


Ingredients

2 cups of fresh basil leaves
1/2 cup of nuts
2 garlic cloves
1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese (grated)
1/2 cup of olive oil
Salt (to taste)


4. Boglonese sauce

Another Italian-signature sauce, with meat, vegetables and red wine. Matches perfectly with spaghetti or lasagna.


Ingredients

500g of ground beef
1 onion (chopped)
2 carrots (chopped)
2 celery stalks (chopped)
2 garlic cloves (minced)
1 can of crushed tomatoes
1/2 cup of red wine
Olive oil
Salt (to taste)
Pepper (to taste)


5. Aglio e Olio

If you're looking for something quick and simple, this sauce made with garlic, olive oil, red pepper and parsley is perfect for your meals.


Ingredients

1/2 cup of olive oil
4 garlic cloves (thinly sliced)
1 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
Fresh parsley (chopped)
Salt (to taste)
