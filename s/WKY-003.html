1. Poached egg toast

Ingredients

2 eggs
2 slices of whole wheat bread


Method

Place both bread slices in the toaster and heat them up for approximately three minutes, or until crunchy. Set them aside.

Fill a saucepan with water and add a teaspoon of vinegar to help the egg whites cook quickly. Crack both eggs into a small bowl, one by one, and slide them into the saucepan once the water is simmering.

Cook for 3 to 5 minutes or until the yolk is soft and the whites have set. Use a slotted spoon to drain excess water and place both eggs on top of the breath slices.

Serve immediately and enjoy.


2. Avocado toast

Ingredients

1 avocado
2 slices of whole wheat bread
Salt
Pepper
Lemon juice (optional)


Method

In a bowl, mash the avocado and season with salt and pepper to the taste. Optionally, freshen up by adding lemon juice. Mix well and set aside.

Place both bread slices in the toaster and heat them up for approximately three minutes, or until crunchy.

Spread the mashed avocado mix into the bread slices evenly.

Serve immediately and enjoy.


3. Chocolate banana toast

Ingredients

1 banana (sliced)
Chocolate syrup
2 slices of whole wheat bread


Method

Place both bread slices in the toaster and heat them up until golden and crunchy.

Spread chocolate syrup evenly on both bread slices. Crown with sliced banana.

Serve immediately and enjoy.
