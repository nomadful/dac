The BLT sandwich that will level up your dinners

Recipes â€¢ 8 min

Ingredients

2 slices of white, whole wheat bread
3 slices of turkey ham
2 lettuce leaves
Half tomato (sliced into 4)


Method

Slice the lettuce leaves into smaller pieces and set them aside.

Heat both slices of bread in the toaster for around two minutes, or until golden and crispy.

Spread a tomato slice on top of the bread to create a saucy base. Add two tomato slices to each bread.

Place the ham on top of the tomatos, and finally crown the inside with lettuce. Wrap the sandwich and cut in half. Serve immediately and enjoy.
