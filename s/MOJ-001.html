1. Scrambled eggs

Ingredients

2 eggs
1 teaspoon of butter
Salt
Black pepper


Method

Crack both eggs in a bowl and season them with salt and black pepper to taste. Mix gently until the yolks and whites are combined.

In a frying pan, melt over medium heat one teaspoon of butter and cover the sides and base of the pan. Once the butter foams, pour the eggs and stir them softly for 3 to 5 minutes.

As soon as the egg is nearly cooked, move the pan away from the heat and let them finish cooking with the remaining pan heat. Serve afterwards.


2. Poached eggs

Ingredients

2 eggs
1 teaspoon of white wine vinegar
Salt
Black pepper


Method

Pour cold water in a small pan and fill just about one third of it and start boiling.

Add the white vinegar and lower the heat.

Crack both eggs, one at a time, inside a small bowl and pour them into the heated water. Gently poach for around three minutes.

Take the eggs out of the pan with the help of a slotted spoon and drain them on top of a kitchen towel

Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve.


3. Fried eggs

Ingredients

2 eggs
2 spoons of olive oil
Salt


Method

Heat a small pan and pour the olive oil inside it.

Crack both eggs into the pan, one at a time, and cook for about one minute.

Using a spoon, pour the remaining olive oil on top of the whites. Lower the heat and let the eggs cook until the whites are set.

Season with salt and serve immediately.


4. Fried egg in toast

Ingredients

1 egg
2 slices of bread
1 spoon of olive oil
Salt

Black pepper


Method

Pour the olive oil into a medium-heated frying pan.

With the help of a glass, make a circular hole in one bread slice. Place the hollow slice on top of the other bread slice, and place them on the pan.

Crack the egg into the hollow bread. Using a spoon, pour the remaining oil on the egg until the white is set.

Once the bread is crispy, season with salt and pepper and serve immediately.
