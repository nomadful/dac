1. Pad Thai

2. Beef Noodle Soup

300g of beef (sliced thinly)
200g of rice noodles
1 onion (sliced)
4 garlic cloves (minced)
4 cups of beef broth
2 tablespoons of soy sauce
1 piece of ginger (sliced)
Fresh cilantro (to taste; for serving)
Lime (to taste)


3. Yakisoba

1 carrot (julienned)
Half onion (sliced)
2 green onions (chopped)
3 tablespoons of yakisoba sauce

Salt (to taste)
Pepper (to taste)
