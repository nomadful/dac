1. Chicken salad

If you've got some leftover chicken fillets, consider this refreshing mix of celery, grapes, nuts and mayonnaise, that's just perfect for a sandwich filling or a super fast and light lunch.


2. Chicken Quesadillas

Flour or corn tortillas packed with shredded chicken, cheese and your favourite vegetable choice, grilled crispy and topped with salsa or tasty guacamole.


3. Fried Chicken and Rice

A quick and filling dish made with stir-fryied chicken, together with rice, vegetables, soy sauce and optionally, scrambled eggs.


4. Chicken Soup

Supercharged chicken broth with simmered leftover chicken, vergetables and herbs. Ideal for warming up during winter days.


5. BBQ Chicken Pizza

While this specific dish may need more time, it's still a super creative meal and a nice twist on pizza using leftover chicken chops tossed in barbeque sauce, topped with cheese and cilantro.
