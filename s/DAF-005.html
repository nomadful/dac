1. Chorizo and beans quesadillas (fried)

Ingredients

2 tortilla wraps
80g of Oaxaca or cheddar cheese
50g of chopped chorizo
80g of mashed beans
Salt
Black pepper
Olive oil


Method

In a shallow bowl, mix the cheese, chorizo, beans, salt and pepper.

Spread the mixture onto the tortillas and fold them in half.

Heat a small amount of olive oil and fry each quesadilla for about three minutes.


2. Chicken quesadillas

Ingredients

4 tortilla wraps
160g of Oaxaca or cheddar cheese
150g of cooked chicken (sliced)
Salt
Black pepper
Basil


Method

Mix together the chicken and cheese, and season with salt, black pepper and basil to taste.

Spread the mix inside each tortilla and fold them in half.

Using a griddle, roast the quesadillas until the cheese is melted and bubbling.

Remove the griddle from the heat and serve the quesadillas.


3. Ham quesadillas

Ingredients

2 tortilla wraps
4 slices of ham
50g Oaxaca or cheddar cheese


Method

Separate the ham and cheese in similar quantities for each quesadilla. Spread some cheese and ham slices into each tortilla and fold it in half.

Using a griddle, roast the quesadillas until the cheese is melted and bubbling, and the tortillas are crispy.

Remove the griddle from the heat and serve the quesadillas.
