The breakfast wrap recipe to get your day started

Recipes â€¢ 12 min

Ingredients

1 tortilla wrap
50g of fried beans
80g of shredded, roasted chicken
1 lettuce leaf
Handful of cilantro


Method

Chop the lettuce leaf and cilantro (if not chopped already). Set them aside.

Heat the tortilla wrap in a flat pan for about one minute.

Spread the fried beans on top of the tortilla, add the chopped lettuce and put the chicken on top. Crown with cilantro.

Finally, roll up the tortilla wrap tightly and heat it in the pan again until it's crunchy. Cut in half and serve immediately.
