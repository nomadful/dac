How to cook the tastiest fish and chips

Ingredients

4 pieces of white fish fillets (cod or haddock)
1 cup of flour (for coating)
1 cup of sparkling water
1 teaspoon of baking powder
Frying oil
4 potatoes (peeled)
Salt (to taste)
Pepper (to taste)


Method

Cut the potatoes into thick chips. Heat frying oil in a large pot and and fry the chips for around 5 minutes, or until they're soft (but not browned).

Remove the chips from the oil and place them on a paper towel to help drain excess oil. Set the chips aside and keep them warm.

In a mixing bowl, add flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Pour the sparkling water gradually until the batter is smooth.

Dip each fish fillet into the batter and coat.

Place the coated fillets into the hot oil and fry for another five minutes, or until they're golden and crispy. Remove from the oil and place in paper towels to drain excess.

Serve immediately and enjoy.
